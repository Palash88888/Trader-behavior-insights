🏆 Insight 1: Fear is Counterintuitively the Most Profitable Sentiment

Despite common assumption that bull markets (Greed) produce the best returns, trades executed during Fear periods generated $3.36M in total PnL — the highest of any sentiment. This suggests sophisticated traders use market fear as a contrarian entry signal, buying dips aggressively when retail sentiment is low. Avg trade size during Fear ($8,041) is also the highest of all sentiments, confirming higher conviction positioning.

📈 Insight 2: Extreme Greed Has the Best Win Rate (89%) but Lower Trade Size

Trades during Extreme Greed have the highest win rate (89%) but with an average trade size of only $2,780 — the lowest of all sentiment zones. This suggests traders are more conservative when the market is euphoric, taking smaller, safer positions. The $2.7M PnL total reflects high frequency rather than high conviction.

📉 Insight 3: Extreme Fear = Highest Risk Zone

Extreme Fear had the most big losses (>$1,000) — 138 trades — and the lowest win rate (76%). The big-loss to big-win ratio is the most adverse here. Traders caught on the wrong side during panic sell-offs face the harshest outcomes. Strategy recommendation: reduce leverage during Extreme Fear; use limit orders rather than market orders.

🚀 Insight 4: December 2024 was an Outlier Month — $3M PnL in a Single Month

December 2024 produced $3.005M in realized PnL from 16,087 trades (win rate: 88.6%). This coincides with Bitcoin's bull run to all-time highs. Most of this activity was during Extreme Greed sentiment. The monthly PnL from Dec 2024 alone exceeds the combined PnL of any prior 6-month period.

💰 Insight 5: Short Positions Outperform Longs in Fear Markets

During Fear periods, Close Short trades generated $1.91M in PnL vs $1.43M for Close Long — a 33% edge. During Extreme Greed, long closures dominate. This aligns with the intuition that professional traders are directional: long during greed, short during fear. Applying this systematically could provide a sentiment-driven edge.

🔥 Insight 6: Top Performing Coins — @107, HYPE, SOL, ETH, BTC

The token @107 led with $2.78M in PnL, followed by HYPE ($1.95M) and SOL ($1.64M). BTC and ETH, while high volume, generated relatively lower PnL — suggesting that altcoin derivatives were the primary alpha source for this cohort. Smart traders concentrated on volatile, higher-beta tokens rather than major assets.
